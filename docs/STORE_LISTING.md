Currency Converter - Convert currencies directly while browsing the web.

Simply highlight any currency amount on any webpage, right-click, and instantly see the converted value in your preferred currency. No more switching tabs or searching for exchange rates!


KEY FEATURES

► Instant Context Menu Conversion
Select any currency amount (like $99.99, €50, or £75) on any webpage, right-click, and see the conversion instantly. Works with 150+ currencies worldwide.

► Smart Currency Detection
Automatically recognizes currency symbols ($, €, £, ¥, ₹) and codes (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY). Even understands written amounts like "100 dollars" or "50 euros".

► Real-Time Exchange Rates
Get accurate, up-to-date exchange rates from trusted providers. Rates are refreshed regularly to ensure accuracy.

► Conversion History
Keep track of all your conversions. Filter by date, search past conversions, and export your history. Never lose track of important conversions again.

► Favorite Currency Pairs
Save your most-used currency pairs for quick access. Perfect for frequent travelers, international shoppers, or remote workers.

► Rate Alerts
Set alerts for specific exchange rates. Get notified when your target rate is reached - perfect for timing your currency exchanges.

► Quick Convert
Add multiple currencies to your quick convert list for instant multi-currency comparisons.


WHY CHOOSE CURRENCY CONVERTER?

✓ Privacy First
All your data stays on your device. We don't collect personal information or track your browsing. Your conversion history and preferences are stored locally.

✓ Efficient Performance
Conversions happen without page reloads. Optimized code ensures minimal impact on your browsing experience.

✓ Works Everywhere
Convert currencies on any website - shopping sites, news articles, travel booking pages, or anywhere you see prices.

✓ No Account Required
Start converting immediately. No sign-up, no login, no hassle.


SUPPORTED CURRENCIES

USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, AUD, CAD, CHF, CNY, INR, MXN, BRL, KRW, SGD, HKD, NOK, SEK, DKK, NZD, ZAR, RUB, TRY, PLN, THB, MYR, IDR, PHP, VND, EGP, AED, SAR, and 120+ more currencies.


HOW TO USE

1. Browse any webpage with currency amounts
2. Highlight the amount you want to convert
3. Right-click and select "Convert Currency"
4. See the instant conversion result!

Or click the extension icon to:
• Change your base currency
• View conversion history
• Manage favorites
• Set up rate alerts
