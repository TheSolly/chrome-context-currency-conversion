Privacy Policy for Currency Converter

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

OVERVIEW

Currency Converter is a browser extension that converts currencies directly from web pages. This policy explains how we handle your information.

WHAT DATA WE STORE

All data is stored LOCALLY on your device only:

• User Preferences - Your selected currencies and display settings
• Conversion History - Record of past conversions for your reference
• Favorite Currency Pairs - Pairs you save for quick access
• Rate Alert Settings - Your configured exchange rate alerts

This data NEVER leaves your device and is NOT sent to any servers.

EXCHANGE RATE API CALLS

To get current exchange rates, the extension connects to these services:
• ExchangeRate-API (exchangerate-api.com)
• Fixer.io (fixer.io)
• CurrencyAPI (currencyapi.com)
• Alpha Vantage (alphavantage.co)
• CurrencyLayer (currencylayer.com)
• Open Exchange Rates (openexchangerates.org)

Only exchange rate data is requested. No personal information is sent.

WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do NOT collect:
• Personal information (name, email, address)
• Browsing history or web activity
• Financial or transaction data
• Location data
• Any data sent to external servers

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED

• contextMenus - Shows "Convert Currency" in right-click menu
• storage - Saves your preferences locally on your device
• activeTab - Reads selected text for currency detection
• notifications - Sends rate alert notifications
• alarms - Checks exchange rates periodically for alerts

DATA SECURITY

• All data stored locally using Chrome's secure Storage API
• Exchange rate requests use HTTPS encryption
• No personal data transmitted externally

YOUR CONTROL

You can clear your data anytime:
1. Open the extension popup
2. Go to History or Settings
3. Use the "Clear" options

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This extension is not intended for children under 13.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

Updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

CONTACT

Questions? Contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.

---

Summary: Currency Converter respects your privacy. Your data stays on your device. We only fetch exchange rates and collect no personal information.
